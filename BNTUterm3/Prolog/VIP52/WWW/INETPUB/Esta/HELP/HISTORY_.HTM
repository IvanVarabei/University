History list

When you see a Down arrow icon to the right of an input box, a history list is attached to the input box.

A history list remembers text that you have already entered. You can edit any item when you have pasted it from the history list.

To invoke the history list click the Down arrow icon or press Alt+Down_arrow.

To choose an item from the history list, click the item to place it in the input box, and press Enter.

To exit the history list without making a selection, click the Down arrow icon again, alternatively press Alt+Down_arrow, or click outside of the input box and history list.
