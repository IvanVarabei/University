Visual Prolog Application Examples

Structure Recognition

Prolog is very good at structure recognition, which makes it easy to create parsers and interpreters. Natural language recognition and sentence analysis are natural applications.

 

Expert Systems

Prologs ability to easily handle complex information has made it one of the few languages suitable for developing expert and knowledge based systems. Many general expert system shells that support high- level knowledge engineering have been developed using the Prolog language. Examples of custom designed Prolog expert system shells are insurance and diagnostic systems and aircraft disposition. The screen below shows the ESTA expert system shell. ESTA is developed in Visual Prolog.

 

 

 

Scheduling and Plan Generation

Scheduling and plan generation are two other areas where Prolog is used extensively, often in situations where traditional methods cannot be used.
