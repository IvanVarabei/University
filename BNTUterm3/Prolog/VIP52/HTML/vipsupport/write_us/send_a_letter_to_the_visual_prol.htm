Send a letter to the Visual Prolog Support team:

  support@visual-prolog.com

Please, include into your letter to the Visual Prolog Support team the following information:

  1. A short informative description of your problem in the Subject field.
    Please be as specific as possible when describing the problem. Do not use general subjects, such as: "Visual Prolog problem", "Help", etc.
  2. A detailed description of the problem in the Message field.
    The information placed here should be sufficient to reproduce the problem on our computers; otherwise, the answer, probably, will be just:
    - "We cannot reproduce the problem!"
    Note that information about VIP serial number , VDE version , Application Expert settings, OS will help us to answer your questions.
    Examples of code illustrating problems are required.
