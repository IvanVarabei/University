Discussion Forum

We are glad to welcome you to the new Visual Prolog discussion forum. 

The forum is un-moderated, and you are free to discuss any Visual Prolog topic. 
Anybody can read the debate, but you need to register if you want to make postings. The purpose of the registration is to avoid
spamming etc. 

Prolog Development Center will follow and participate as much as possible in the debates. But we can not make any "answer
guarantee", this is mainly your forum. 

This forum will receive all major announcements from Prolog Development Center.
