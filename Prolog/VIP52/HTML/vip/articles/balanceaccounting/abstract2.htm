DEVELOPMENT OF AN ANALYSIS SYSTEM FOR COMPANIES' FINANCIAL STATEMENT ANALYSIS

Abstract

Balance Consulting Ltd is a Finnish company specialising in company and industry analysis. The authors have participated in making an expert system for making company ratings and verbal analyses based on financial statements. The system creates the possibility of providing a cost-effective analysis compared to an analysis done by an experienced analyst.

Keywords:

Prolog, Financial Statement analysis, Company rating
