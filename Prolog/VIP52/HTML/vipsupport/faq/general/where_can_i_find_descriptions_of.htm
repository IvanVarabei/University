Where can I find descriptions of error messages I get?

Our Help often contains extended descriptions of reported errors.

The shortest way to display such description is:

  1. Choose Help menu item, or just press F1 to activate online Help (VIP.HLP file);
  2. Click on the "Search" button, or just type 'S';
  3. Type error number, for example, 1025;
  4. The correspondent topic will be displayed.
